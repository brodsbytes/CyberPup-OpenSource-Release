CyberPup Tiered Health Check Mapping

Level 1 – CyberPup Scout 🐾
Beginner
Goal: Establish a strong baseline of cyber hygiene in the least time possible.

1. Password Security & Authentication
Check 1: Creating Strong Passwords & Passphrases
    • Why: Weak or reused passwords cause most hacks.
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ My passwords are 12+ characters
        ◦ I use unique passwords on each account
        ◦ I avoid personal info like names & birthdays
        ◦ I use a passphrase of 4+ random words for important accounts
Check 2: Prioritising High-Value Accounts
    • Why: Banking & email are the keys to everything else.
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ My bank accounts have MFA enabled
        ◦ My primary email has MFA & a strong passphrase
Check 3: Using a Password Manager
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I installed a password manager
        ◦ I stored 3+ logins in it
        ◦ I enabled biometrics for access
Check 4: Setting Up MFA (2FA)
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I setup or use an Authenticator App
        ◦ MFA enabled on email
        ◦ MFA enabled on bank accounts
        ◦ Backup codes stored securely
Check 5: Checking if My Data Was Breached
    • Practice: Enter email → breach API
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Checked my email on XposedOrNot
        ◦ If breaches, take to guide in Insights on what to do
        ◦ Changed any leaked passwords
🎉 Badge: Password Pro

2. Device & Network Security
Check 1: Screen Lock & Auto-Lock
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ My phone auto-locks after inactivity
        ◦ I use a strong PIN/passphrase
Check 2: Enable Remote Lock & Wipe
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Remote lock is enabled
        ◦ Remote wipe is enabled
Check 3: Updating Devices & Apps
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Auto-updates are ON for OS & apps
        ◦ I updated all devices this month
Check 4: Bluetooth & Auto-Wi-Fi
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Bluetooth is off when unused
        ◦ Auto-connect to new Wi-Fi is disabled
Check 5: Avoid Public Charging Stations
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I only use my own charger & cable
🎉 Badge: Device Defender

3. Data Protection & Backups
Check 1: Setting Up Cloud Backup
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Cloud backup is enabled on my phone
        ◦ Cloud backup is enabled on my computer
Check 2: Local Backup Option
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I back up to an external drive
        ◦ I tested a restore process
🎉 Badge: Data Guardian

4. Phishing & Scam Awareness
Check 1: Recognising Scam Tactics
    • ✅ Interactive exercise:
        ◦ I know authority/urgency/emotion/scarcity triggers
        ◦ I pause before clicking any suspicious link
Check 2: Reporting Scams
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I know how to report in email client, or to local scamwatch
        ◦ I've saved the reporting link/number
🎉 Badge: Scam Spotter

5. Online Privacy & Social Media
Check 1: Think Before You Share
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ I avoid posting DOB, address, employer, or school publicly
        ◦ I review posts for exploitable info before publishing
Check 2: Privacy Settings Review
    • ✅ Checklist:
        ◦ Social media privacy settings are reviewed & adjusted
        ◦ I limit profile visibility on professional networks
🎉 Badge: Privacy Guardian
✅ Badge Earned: CyberPup Scout 🐾

Level 2 – CyberPup Watchdog 👁️
Expanding Your Defences
Goal: Move from "safe enough" to "hard target" by closing gaps cybercriminals often exploit after basic protections are in place.

1. Lock Down All Your Accounts
Mission: Every account you own should be as hard to break into as your bank.
Actions:
    • Turn on Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all accounts, including:
        ◦ Shopping/payment (eBay, Amazon, PayPal)
        ◦ All social media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, WhatsApp)
        ◦ Government & services (myGov, Apple ID, Uber, iCloud)
    • Upgrade any password without MFA to a unique strong passphrase (4+ random words, at least 15 characters).
    • Check for breaches:
        ◦ Visit Have I Been Pwned
        ◦ If in a breach → change passphrase + enable MFA immediately.
🎉 Badge: Account Vault

2. Master Your Password Manager
Mission: Your password manager becomes your brain's secure vault.
Actions:
    • Enable MFA on your password manager.
    • Create the strongest passphrase you've ever made for the "master password".
    • Add login details every time you use an account.
    • Remove any old or duplicate entries.
🎉 Badge: Vault Level

3. Beat Public Wi-Fi Risks
Mission: Treat public Wi-Fi like a suspicious stranger.
Actions:
    • Turn off auto-connect for unknown networks.
    • Confirm the official hotspot name before connecting.
    • Avoid sending sensitive info (banking, passwords) on public Wi-Fi — use mobile data instead.
    • Remove stored public Wi-Fi networks after use.
    • Turn off Bluetooth & Wi-Fi when not in use.
🎉 Badge: Public Wi-Fi Ninja

4. Secure Your Home Wi-Fi Router
Mission: Turn your home router into a fortress.
Actions:
    • Change the default router admin password (not just the Wi-Fi password).
    • Ensure WPA2 or WPA3 encryption is enabled.
    • Update router firmware to the latest version.
    • Enable the router’s built-in firewall.
    • Turn off WPS (Wi-Fi Protected Setup).
🎉 Badge: Router Fortress

5. Securely Retire Old Devices
Mission: Make old devices useless to anyone else.
Actions:
    • Backup and transfer important data.
    • Factory reset to erase all personal info.
    • Remove SIM, SD cards, or USB drives.
    • Physically destroy removable media if no longer used.
🎉 Badge: Cyber Recycler

6. Stay Ahead of Malware & Ransomware
Mission: Stop threats before they can touch your files.
Actions:
    • Turn on real-time protection in your antivirus.
    • Enable ransomware protection (Windows: Controlled Folder Access).
    • Keep antivirus updated.
    • Backup regularly.
🎉 Badge: Threat Shield

7. Reduce Your Digital Footprint
Mission: Give cybercriminals less to work with.
Actions:
    • Google your name → remove personal info where possible.
    • Delete unused online accounts.
    • Remove personal details from public sites.
    • Avoid posting info that could answer security questions.
🎉 Badge: Shadow Mode

8. Secure Apps & Browser Extensions
Mission: Only run trusted, updated tools.
Actions:
    • Enable automatic app updates.
    • Only download from official app stores.
    • Uninstall unused apps/extensions.
    • Review app permissions — remove anything unnecessary.
🎉 Badge: App Gatekeeper
✅ Badge Earned: CyberPup Watchdog 👁️

Level 3 – CyberPup Guardian 🛡
Maximum Protection
Goal: Implement expert-level defences used by security-conscious professionals to stay safe against the most persistent and sophisticated threats.

1. Advanced Account & Identity Protection
Mission: Go beyond MFA — make account takeovers nearly impossible.
Actions:
    • Use hardware security keys (e.g., YubiKey, Feitian) for critical accounts (email, banking, work).
    • Enable account recovery protections (backup emails/phone numbers, non-guessable security questions).
    • Turn on login notifications for suspicious activity.
    • Freeze your credit file (if available in your country).
🎉 Badge: Fort Knox

2. Advanced Device Security Hardening
Mission: Make your devices resilient against even targeted attacks.
Actions:
    • Turn on full disk encryption (FileVault, BitLocker, Android/iOS encryption).
    • Enable secure boot in BIOS/UEFI settings.
    • Remove admin rights from your daily-use account; use a separate admin account for installs only.
    • Configure a firewall to block unnecessary incoming connections.
🎉 Badge: Ironclad

3. Advanced Network Security
Mission: Control your network like a pro.
Actions:
    • Change default router admin credentials.
    • Use WPA3 encryption for Wi-Fi.
    • Disable WPS (Wi-Fi Protected Setup).
    • Create a guest Wi-Fi network for visitors & IoT devices.
    • Enable network-level ad/tracker blocking (Pi-hole, DNS filtering).
🎉 Badge: Network Commander

4. Threat Monitoring & Alerts
Mission: Detect suspicious activity before it becomes a problem.
Actions:
    • Sign up for ACSC Alert Service or your local cyber alert system.
    • Enable security alerts on all major accounts (Google, Microsoft, Apple, banking).
    • Use a personal threat monitoring service (Aura, Identity Guard, or government options).
    • Regularly check Have I Been Pwned for new breaches.
🎉 Badge: Cyber Scout

5. Secure Remote Work & Cloud Use
Mission: Keep your online work and storage airtight.
Actions:
    • Use end-to-end encrypted messaging (Signal, Wire).
    • Enable link expiry & password protection for cloud file shares.
    • Avoid storing sensitive data in free, unencrypted cloud services.
    • Enable offline access passwords for synced files.
🎉 Badge: Cloud Guardian

6. Incident Response Plan
Mission: Be ready to act instantly if things go wrong.
Actions:
    • Write down the emergency contact list (bank, telco, government cyber hotline).
    • Have a step-by-step incident checklist (account compromise, device theft, ransomware).
    • Store backups offline or on an encrypted external drive.
    • Test your backups twice a year.
🎉 Badge: Rapid Responder

7. Advanced Privacy & Footprint Reduction
Mission: Make yourself invisible to trackers and snoops.
Actions:
    • Use a privacy-focused browser (Brave, Firefox hardening, Safari private mode).
    • Use a privacy VPN (Mullvad, ProtonVPN, IVPN — no logs).
    • Run regular OS & social media privacy audits.
    • Replace high-risk services with secure alternatives (ProtonMail, Nextcloud).
🎉 Badge: Ghost Mode
✅ Badge Earned: CyberPup Guardian 🛡
